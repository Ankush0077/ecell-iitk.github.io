The work of E-Cell spans across diverse fields and we are sure that you will find your interest in one of them!

Secretary, Events

You will get a hands-on experience of managing and structuring an event, learn team management and get ample opportunities for one-on-one interaction with leading entrepreneurs. Events team is responsible for organizing and structuring all the major events like eSummit, TEDx, Startup Master Class, and many others.
